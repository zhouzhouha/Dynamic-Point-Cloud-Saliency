Visual Saliency of Dynamic Point Cloud

Research on visual attention modeling for 3D point cloud data in both task-free and task-dependent scenarios

Introduction

This research explores visual saliency in dynamic point clouds, examining how humans allocate attention when viewing 3D visual content. We present two complementary studies: one focusing on task-dependent visual saliency and another comparing task-free and task-dependent scenarios.

Understanding visual attention in 3D environments is crucial for various applications including compression, quality assessment, and computer graphics.
